About Me

Telling stories through motion

I'm Omisha Soni — a video editor and designer with over five years of experience turning raw footage into compelling visual narratives. From brand films and commercials to music videos and social content, I bring a meticulous eye for detail and a deep understanding of pacing, color, and rhythm to every project.

My approach blends technical precision with creative intuition. I believe that every cut, transition, and color grade should serve the story. Whether it's a fast-paced commercial or an emotive documentary, I craft edits that resonate with audiences and elevate brands.
